# Release Notes

Date: 2026-04-13

## What this public repo shows

- A clear front door for the OpenIA project.
- A concise explanation of the daily dev log pipeline.
- A public link to the reusable `daily-dev-log-skill`.
- A simple map of how the pieces fit together.

## Why this version is useful

The goal is not to dump every internal file. The goal is to make the project understandable in under a minute and invite people to try the reusable part first.

## What is intentionally omitted

- Local router logs and raw diagnostics output.
- Machine-specific caches and temporary files.
- Personal working notes that do not help a new visitor understand the project.
